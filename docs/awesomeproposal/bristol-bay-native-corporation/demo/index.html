How it sits in your operation

Three steps, and a human on the trigger the whole way.

Step one

A writer asks

A solicitation lands anywhere in the 22-subsidiary family. The writer asks Capture Memory in plain language, the same question they would ask a colleague who had been at Bristol Bay for twenty years.

Step two

The system finds and cites

Access control clears the writer first, then retrieval returns prior text they are allowed to see, each passage stamped with the winning entity and contract. The system finds and cites. It does not invent.

Step three

The writer decides

The writer verifies each source and chooses what to reuse. Every compliance-graded word stays human-owned. Nothing is auto-drafted and nothing is submitted by the tool, ever.

The guarantee you can see. The permission boundary is deterministic and sits outside the model, so a boundary failure can never be a model failure. Try the restricted question in the console above, the gate returns "not cleared" and retrieval never runs. That is the design spec, not a setting.
