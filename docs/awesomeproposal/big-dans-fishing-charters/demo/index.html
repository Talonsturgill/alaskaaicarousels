Before you play it

What is real, and what is invented for this demo

Your own site already tells guests what to do when a date will not open online.

For dates not showing availability online, your best bet is to call Sarah at 907-598-3391.

From bigdansfishing.com, your own availability page.

The scenarios below show what changes once an AI front desk sits in front of that one line. They also show what stays exactly the same.

The dates are invented. The names are invented. So is every boat status you will see below. Nothing here is a real guest, and nothing here touches your real calendar or your real phone line.

Not built into this version

  • Answer the phone. That is a later phase, gated on confirming your line can support it.
  • Reschedule a trip around weather on its own. That risk is too high for any small operator to hand to a model today.
  • Take a payment or make the call on hunting transport. Both stay with Dan and Sarah, always.
  • Answer questions your own site already answers. This is not a general FAQ bot bolted onto your homepage.

How this fits your operation

In plain words

1

A guest asks

A guest asks the chat widget a question on bigdansfishing.com, any hour of the day.

2

The calendar decides

The assistant checks your real FareHarbor calendar. If what they want is open exactly as it shows online, it books it. The confirmation that goes out is the same one your calendar already generates today.

3

Dan and Sarah decide the rest

Off-calendar. Cross-location. Weather-related. Safety-adjacent. Any one of those stops the assistant cold. It writes up exactly what it knows. Then it sends that straight to Dan and Sarah. That is the same handoff that happens today, just organized before it lands.

The guarantee

Nobody gets replaced here. Sarah and Dan still make every call this assistant cannot make on its own. It cannot book hunting transport. It cannot reschedule around weather. It cannot take a payment on a judgment call. Those stay human, on purpose.
