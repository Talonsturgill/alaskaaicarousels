Every compliance matrix needs your proposal manager's sign-off before any draft gets written. Every time, with no exception built into the tool.

What this demo is, and is not

  • This is a scripted demonstration, built by Alaska AI, of the build described in your Field Study. It is not a live system and it is not connected to anything.
  • The solicitation and requirement text in Steps 1 through 4 are illustrative samples built for this demonstration. No real UIC or Bowhead solicitation, proposal, or contract data was used anywhere in this file.
  • The build is scoped as a fixed, checkpointed workflow, not an autonomous agent. The human sign-off on the compliance matrix is mandatory and cannot be skipped, that gate is the whole point.
  • At launch, the corpus this tool draws from is scoped to non-CUI, public content only, and to one pilot subsidiary. No CUI-classified content is processed by this build until your own compliance team clears an infrastructure path for that, per your Field Study.
  • This tool never drafts DCAA-sensitive pricing narratives and never drafts the Technical Volume. Both stay entirely with your people, exactly as scoped in your Field Study.
  • The Field Study attached alongside this demonstration carries the full plan, the honest ROI range, the risks, and the open questions. This file is the walkthrough, that document is the record.
