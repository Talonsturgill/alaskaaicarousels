## DECK HEADER

### Thesis
In one Arctic month, Alaska is deciding whether to become the AI industry's
engine room, and of the four rooms where it is being decided, only one, the
DNR comment docket, has a door the public can walk through, and it closes
July 17.

### Document title (<= 60 chars)
"Alaska's AI Land Rush: Four Rooms, One Open Door" (47 chars)

### Arc (9 slides; emotional temperature in brackets)
1. HOOK cover: the revolt, 500 vs fewer than 12 [cold shock]
2. Context: what and where, the Stak campus on the Slope [orienting]
3. Point/data: the scale, 1 GW beats urban Alaska's whole grid [alarm]
4. BREATHER: the tundra footprint, gravel and gas over permafrost [somber, wide]
5. Point: the record, Alaskans in their own words vs "form letters" [human, defiant]
6. DATA PIVOT / widen: one of four decisions this month [the turn: "oh, it is bigger"]
7. Point: the federal room, leasing with no comment box [unease]
8. SYNTHESIS / position: four rooms, one open door [clarifying argument]
9. CLOSE: the open door closes July 17, single debate ask [call to judgment]
