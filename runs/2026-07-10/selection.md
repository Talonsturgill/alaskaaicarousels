Right now, three Alaska volcanoes are in elevated status at once (Great Sitkin erupting lava, Watch/Orange; Kupreanof and Shishaldin restless, Advisory/Yellow, per the July 3, 2026 AVO update). This is Alaska building the tools that keep those skies safe, and teaching the nation's other observatories to use them.
