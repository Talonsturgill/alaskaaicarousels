# STORYBOARD — Carousel No. 15 — "Governed First" (2026-07-23)

## DECK HEADER

**Document title (PDF, <=60 chars):** Governed First, Alaska Native Health and AI

**Thesis:** Alaska's tribal health system is adopting clinical machine learning
fast and governing it first, on the Tribe's own authority (Indigenous data
sovereignty), deciding what is allowed to run on Alaska Native health data. It is
a real, documented, early approach, and worth watching.

**Arc (9 slides):**
1. HOOK, the Tribe decides what AI runs (rendered gold authority seal).
2. DEFINITION, sovereignty means authority over the data, not just privacy.
3. STAKES, why the data is life and death here (medevac 40x, 80% roadless).
4. WHAT IS RUNNING, one tool live (AI scribe) vs one proposed (medevac model).
5. THE RULESET, CARE + FAIR, the rules the seal enforces (keepable).
6. THE AUTHORS, Alaska Native institutions convened it (31-person snapshot).
7. ADOPTION, near-zero to substantial, drawn honestly (keepable chart).
8. HONEST TURN, this rests on one peer-reviewed paper, dated (58e evidence tags).
9. CLOSE, adopt fast, govern first, single debate ask + brand fixtures.
Emotional temperature: affirmative and proud S1 to S6, sobering honest turn at S8,
resolve at S9. The honest-limit TURN lands at S8 (the 8-of-9 slot).

a definition, the Alaska stakes, the deployed-vs-proposed reality, the ruleset,
