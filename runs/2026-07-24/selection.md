## WINNER: The North Slope Stak Energy AI data-center public revolt (story_id STAK)

**Working thesis.** Alaska's single largest AI-infrastructure decision, a roughly
one-square-mile gas-fired compute campus on state land near Deadhorse, drew a
public rejection of more than 500 comments to fewer than a dozen in support, and
now the whole question rests with one state office. The deck is about that gap,
the mismatch between a machine proposed at continental scale and a public that
overwhelmingly said no, and about what "consent" means once the comment room has
closed and one office holds the pen.

   wall of public comment; 350 to 500 million cubic feet of gas per day.
3. Tangibility: a real pending government decision with a hard, just-passed peg.
4. Send-to-a-coworker: the loudest AI backlash in the state right now.
