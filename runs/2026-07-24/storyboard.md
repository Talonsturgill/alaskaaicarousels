**Thesis.** When more than 500 Alaskans object to the North Slope's largest AI
data center and fewer than a dozen approve, the honest response is to grade the
objections, not just amplify them: the loudest fear (your power bill) is the
weakest argument, the strongest ones (public land for 50 years, gravel on the
tundra, a gigawatt of gas) hold, and all of it still rests with one state office
that calls the record "form letters lacking substance."

clarity (5 to 7) -> quiet gut-punch (8) -> open question (9).
