An Alaska Native-owned firm just won a $24.96M Navy contract for autonomous stealth boats. They will be built in Oregon.

ReconCraft is Alaska-incorporated, Anchorage-based, and Native-owned. On July 8 the Navy handed it $24,960,408, sole source, for autonomous low-profile vessels, boats that barely break the surface.

The Navy did not compete it. It went sole source through the SBA 8(a) program, built to route federal contracts to Native-owned firms. The boats will be welded in Clackamas, Oregon.

So what is "Alaska" in this boom? An ownership stake and an address, not a shipyard. A real Native win, and most of what Alaska's slice looks like now.
