## Thesis (fair, structural, brand-voiced)

contracting instrument: Native-owned 8(a) status and an Anchorage business
address. That won a marquee Navy autonomy contract sole-source. The steel is
cut in Clackamas, Oregon. Both are true and both matter. The deck names the
gap between Alaska's PAPER footprint (ownership, set-aside status, HQ) and its
PHYSICAL footprint (where the boats are actually welded), without diminishing a
genuine Alaska Native enterprise milestone or the legitimacy of the 8(a)
program.
