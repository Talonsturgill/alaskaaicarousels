# STORYBOARD — Alaska.Ai Carousel No. 7 — 2026-07-14

## DECK HEADER

**Working title (document title):** The Anchorage Address
**Thesis:** In the autonomous-defense build-out, "Alaska" can be a
contracting instrument, not a shipyard. An Alaska-incorporated,
Anchorage-headquartered, Native-owned SBA 8(a) firm won a $24,960,408
sole-source Navy contract for autonomous stealth vessels. The boats will be
welded in Clackamas, Oregon. Both facts are real; the deck names the gap
between Alaska's paper footprint and its physical one, fairly.

**Arc (8 slides):** paradox (cover) -> the deal (payoff) -> the boat
(hero) -> the autonomy, honestly -> the mechanism (8a) -> the geography
split -> the reframe (breather) -> the debatable close.

**Slide-count rationale:** 8 slides. Tight single-metaphor story; the
waterline carries it. 8 is the save-optimized band (8 to 10) and there is
no filler beat.

**Solo-run note:** treatment-director, copywriter, pixel-critic, flow-critic,
and scorer subagents are unavailable (account weekly limit). The showrunner
authored the treatment, will self-review renders by reading the PNGs and
thumbs, and will score honestly against the rubric. Rendered-ladder GL/raymarch
heroes are scoped to a high-craft Canvas-2D orthographic hero for reliability;
disclosed in the email.
