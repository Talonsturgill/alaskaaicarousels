# Selection — Carousel No. 9 — 2026-07-17

## DECISION: Quinhagak / Nalaquq — the village that trains its own machines

**Story.** In Quinhagak (Yukon-Kuskokwim Delta), Nalaquq LLC, the Alaska
Native-owned tech subsidiary of the village corporation Qanirtuuq, has
formalized a drone-piloting training pipeline (a 14-week FAA Part 107 course,
DOT-grant-backed) and, on June 9, 2026, hosted a dozen trainees from across
rural Alaska. Reported by KYUK and Alaska News Nightly on July 14, 2026
(in-window). The same Native-owned company earlier built its own machine
vision: a custom Convolutional Neural Network (deep learning, COCO-pretrained)
that detects small aluminum-hulled boats for search and rescue from
consumer-grade drones, and a polarized-lens UAV pipeline that tracks and counts
migrating salmon (peer-reviewed JCMC paper; the SR page lists "Automated Salmon
Counts").

**Thesis (one sentence).** In a roadless Yup'ik region where search-and-rescue
calls come weekly and outside operators cannot read the airspace, a village
corporation is training its own pilots and running its own neural network, so
the community owns not just the drone but the machine that watches its land,
waters, and people.

**Why it wins (rubric criteria in order).**
1. Concrete Alaska impact, maximal: named village, named people, subsistence,
   SAR, a real Native-owned company with a real CNN. Off the charts on Alaska
   authenticity.
2. Visual potential: roadless delta as a network, drone flight paths from a
   single village node, thermal-signature counts, the Kanektok salmon line, the
   Nunalleq heritage-erosion origin. Strong story-art fusion.
3. Tangibility: a 19-year-old learning to fly; weekly SAR calls; a boat found by
   a neural net. Human and physical.
4. Would an Alaskan send this to a coworker? Yes; it is a pride-and-sovereignty
   story, not another data-center fight.

  (c15/c17). Cite them as built, prior work, not as part of the June training.

  (c13, c17) are drone/thermal applications with NO machine-learning evidence.
  Do NOT frame either as AI. Reindeer thermal imaging = a human reading heat.

  unnamed) with the 4-community November cohort (Quinhagak, Eek, Goodnews,
  Platinum). They are two separate facts (c3 vs c7).
